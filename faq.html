Q: Are these conversations private/confidential?
A: Yes! Warmbot does not take any of your personal information while chatting with you. You deserve a safe space to express your feelings and feel supported without having to worry about your privacy.


Q: Is there any way to retrieve past conversations with Warmbot?
A: Unfortunately, we made the decision not to save conversation history in order to preserve the integrity of our promise to protect user privacy.


Q: How does Warmbot work?
A: Warmbot is an AI chatbot that has been trained on an abundance of mental health data to listen to your feelings and respond in a way that will hopefully provide you with the most support!
